1) Tina the Turtle's race with the Wind

Tina the turtle wanted to race the wind, but she was just too slow.

So she tied a leaf to her back and waited for a breeze.

When it came, she slid across the grass with a big smile.

"I may be slow," she giggled, "but I sure can glide!"

Even if you can't do things the usual way, creativity can help you find your own path to joy.

3) Bobby the Bear's Blanket Hunt

Bobby the bear couldn't sleep without his favorite blue blanket, but it was nowhere to be found.

He searched under leaves, behind trees, and even asked the owls!

At last, he found it tucked under his little sister, who'd borrowed it for a nap

Bobby smiled, tucked her in, and grabbed a cozy leaf pile instead.

Sometimes sharing brings more warmth than a blanket ever could.

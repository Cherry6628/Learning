4) Benny the Monkey's Banana Mix-Up

Benny the monkey was in a hurry and accidentally packed socks instead of bananas for lunch.

At noon, he took a big bite and screamed, "These bananas are extra chewy!"

His friends laughed so hard, they traded their real bananas for his fuzzy socks.

From that day on, Benny became the jungle's official lunchbox checker.

Always double-check your lunch - or be ready to trade with a smile !

5) Timmy the Turtle's Too-Late Talent Show

Timmy the turtle practiced his dance for the forest talent show - but kept saying, "I'll start in five minutes."

He finally showed up... right after everyone had packed up and gone home!

The squirrels gave him a leaf medal anyway - for "Best Solo Show Performed to Empty Stumps."

From then on, Timmy set his alarm five minutes before he said he would!

If you snooze, you might miss the fun - so don't wait till the last minute!

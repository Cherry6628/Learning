Tasty South Indian Dosa

  1. Batter Preparation
    • Ingredients:
      • 1 cup parboiled rice
      • 1 cup raw rice
      • 1/2 cup urad dal
      • 2 tbsp chana dal
      • 1/4 tsp fenugreek seeds
      • Salt and water
    • Steps:
      1. Rinse everything well.
      2. Soak all together for 6 hours.
      3. Grind to smooth thick batter.
      4. Ferment overnight in warm place.
      5. Mix salt next morning and stir gently.
  2. Coconut Chutney
    • Ingredients:
      • 1/2 cup coconut
      • 2 tbsp roasted chana dal
      • 1 green chili, ginger, salt
    • Steps:
      1. Grind with water into smooth paste.
      2. Optional: add mustard and curry leaves tempering.
  3. Final Dosa
    • Heat pan and spread batter.
    • Drizzle oil and cook till crisp.
    • Place masala, fold and serve hot.

Soft South Indian Idli

  1. Batter Preparation
    • Ingredients:
      • 2 cups idli rice
      • 1/2 cup urad dal
      • 1/4 cup poha
      • 1/4 tsp methi seeds
      • Salt and water
    • Steps:
      1. Soak rice, dal, poha separately.
      2. Grind dal and poha smooth.
      3. Grind rice slightly coarse.
      4. Mix and ferment overnight.
      5. Add salt before steaming.
  2. Steaming
    • Grease idli plates.
    • Pour batter 3/4 full in each mold.
    • Steam for 10-12 mins.
    • Cool a bit, then remove and serve.
  3. Side Chutney
    • Ingredients:
      • Coconut, chana dal, chili, ginger
    • Steps:
      1. Grind smooth with water.
      2. Add optional tempering if desired.
  4. Tips
    • Use wet grinder for better results.
    • Keep batter warm to ferment well.
    • Don't stir too much after rising.
